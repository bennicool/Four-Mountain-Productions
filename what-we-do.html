What We Do

From concept to final delivery — films, commercials, and stills.

Four Mountain Productions is a vital and creative energy, looking to be one of the key players in the future of the South African film and television industry.

OUR MISSION

Despite Africa’s advances, we still remain a continent cloaked in the mystery and magic it held centuries ago. A culture steeped in myth and legend gave birth to a tradition of storytelling which began the moment man uttered his first words. Our goal is to bring Africa to life in a way which is both relevant and appealing locally and internationally.

OUR VISION

We are working towards a future where the African film and television industry becomes a global player. In any project we believe in embracing our diverse cultures, allowing us the possibility to surf the limitless oceans of imagination.
